My PortFolio

This portfolio focuses on insight and transparency to ensure a clear presentation of the work. A consistent visual approach supports readability and structure throughout the design. The layout is designed with attention to detail and visual balance. Overall, the theme aims to create a clear and accessible overview of the projects.

The moodboard establishes the visual direction of the portfolio by defining atmosphere, color, and tone. It focuses on insight and transparency through controlled contrasts and cinematic references.

The style tile translates the conceptual direction into concrete design elements such as typography, color palette, and UI components. The desaturated tones and accents was a choice to try and reinforce the cinematic and focused visual approach.

The front page was developed using Adobe Illustrator. The background pupil was used as a visual element to support the concept of insight and transparency. Grid lines were also intentionally retained in the final website to reinforce the theme of transparency and structural clarity.

Deciding how to present my previous work was a challenging process. The goal was to create a cinematic experience through subtle animations that keep the user engaged and focused. Small visual components, such as the orange half circle, were designed to reinforce the overall atmosphere of the portfolio.

The video was produced in Adobe Premiere Pro to support the cinematic identity of the portfolio. The result is a dynamic extension of the visual theme, reinforcing mood and storytelling.
