Virksomheds Site

Working in a group, we redesigned an existing company website based on research, user insights, and structured content. The project combined UX principles, visual consistency, and hands-on frontend workflows.

Early in the project, we conducted visual research and gathered inspiration to define the overall direction. This included analysing competitors, existing brand elements, and user expectations. The research formed the foundation for later design decisions and ensured a coherent visual expression.

The project was developed through close group collaboration using GitHub repositories. Version control allowed us to work efficiently, share code, and document progress. The final solution was presented and documented as a complete redesign of a real company website.

Based on the research, we developed wireframes to structure content and user flow. The layouts focused on clarity, hierarchy, and usability across devices. This step ensured a solid connection between user needs and the final design.

We produced original content by filming and photographing inside the McKorman store. Images were edited and color-graded to match the defined style tile and brand identity. This ensured consistency across the site and supported a more authentic user experience.

We carried out qualitative research, including an interview with the assistant manager at McKorman. The interview provided valuable insights into the business, target audience, and daily operations. These insights directly influenced both content structure and design priorities.
